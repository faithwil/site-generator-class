ABSTRACT

Rapper / Singer

Kevin Abstract is a multifaceted artist recognized for his innovative approach to music and visual storytelling. As a rapper, singer, and songwriter, he defies traditional genre boundaries, blending elements of hip-hop, pop, and alternative music. His introspective lyrics delve into personal experiences, touching on themes of identity, sexuality, and the human condition.

Beyond his music, Kevin is a visionary director and visual artist, known for his bold and captivating visuals. He has directed many of his own music videos, infusing them with cinematic storytelling and striking imagery. His creative vision extends to his album covers and overall aesthetic, which often feature vibrant colors and surreal elements.
