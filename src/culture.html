Concert Advice+

Concerts offer some of the most memorable experiences, whether they leave a lasting impression for positive or negative reasons. Here are some tid bits from myself & friends.

1

Anticipate Audience Behavior

Some people aren't aware of their surroundings or have the best judgment

2

Talk To Other Fans

You may have more in common than you think

3

PLEASE Drink Water

Cotton mouth at a concert is wild

1

Plan For Anythhing & Everything

Food, transportation, attire. You just never know what could happen

2

Don't Let Big Crowds Pressure You

Just because you see a long line doesn't mean you need to spend $100 on a shirt! Save money!

3

Bring Earplugs

Concerts can be very loud. I use my Loop earplugs sometimes

1

Search The Setlist

Concerts are fun when you know the words!

2

Hydrate

The pit can get hot

1

Bring A Buddy

Going with company you know & enjoy can enhance any concert experience

2

Pack A Portable Charger

My phone was on its last life. Don't make that mistake

3

Dress On Theme

Try to join the theme of the concert!

1

Bring A Good Amount Of Money

If you do splurge, merch & food are usually expensive

2

Be Prepared

It's better to be prepared than sorry

1

Take In The Overall Experience

Try to take in the experience of the concert, not just the show enjoy the venue, the people you go with, etc. Those can enhance your experience

1

Stand Your Ground

Don’t let people break your boundaries

2

Realize You May Be Uncomfortable

Long lines, uncomfortable clothes / shoes, unpredictable weather, etc

3

Have FUN!

Don't be afraid to let loose and enjoy the moment - no one else cares
